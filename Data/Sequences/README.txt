Data used for analyses:

- rsob160315supp3.fasta: BUB (BUB1), MAD (BUBR1), and MADBUB (preduplication ancestors) sequences used in Tromer et al. Open Biol 2016 (http://dx.doi.org/10.1098/rsob.160315) 

- rsob160315supp4.fasta: Domain annotation for BUB (BUB1), MAD (BUBR1), and MADBUB (preduplication ancestors) sequences used in Tromer et al. Open Biol 2016 (http://dx.doi.org/10.1098/rsob.160315)

- Human_BUB1B_orthologues_2018_12_05.fa: Sequences of orthologs of the human BUBR1 sequence downloaded from Ensembl Orthologs on December 5th, 2018.

- Human_BUB1_orthologues_2018_12_05.fa: Sequences of orthoogs of the human BUB1 sequence downloaded from Ensembl Orthologs on December 5th, 2018.
